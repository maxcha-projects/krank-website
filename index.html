How Scores Work

The factors behind every KRANK score

📊 The KRANK Score
KRANK scores are built by combining five dimensions of influence — how far a celebrity reaches on social media, how often they appear in traditional media, how many brand partnerships they hold, how active their fanbase is, and how much public interest they generate. Each dimension is evaluated and combined into a single authoritative score out of 100.
Score Factors
WHAT GOES INTO EVERY KRANK SCORE
FACTORWHAT IT MEASURES
Social ReachTotal follower count and growth across all major platforms
Media PresenceFrequency and scale of TV, magazine, and news appearances
Brand InfluenceNumber and prestige of active brand partnerships
Fan EngagementStreaming activity, voting participation, and fan community strength
Search InterestReal-time public curiosity and trending momentum
