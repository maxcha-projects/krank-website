How Scores Work

The formula behind every KRANK score

🧮 The KRANK Score Formula
KRANK Score =
  (Social Followers Score × 0.35) +
  (Media Appearances Score × 0.25) +
  (Brand Deals Score × 0.20) +
  (Fan Activity Score × 0.15) +
  (Search Trend Score × 0.05)
